StoryArc Studio Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026.

StoryArc Studio is an approval-gated media workflow. It uses TikTok authorization only for an authorized creator to review or publish videos selected for that creator's account.

The service may process TikTok account identifiers, display name, OAuth tokens, granted posting permissions, upload status, generated video files, captions, review decisions, and operational logs. It never requests or stores a TikTok password.

OAuth credentials are stored server-side and are not placed in public video metadata. Generated media and its approved posting metadata are sent to TikTok only after the account operator explicitly authorizes the export.

Information is not sold. An authorized user may revoke access through TikTok account settings. Revocation stops new API actions.

Operational records are retained only as needed for security, deduplication, upload recovery, and compliance, then deleted according to the operator's retention settings.
